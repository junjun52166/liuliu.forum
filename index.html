About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where players face endless waves of increasingly dangerous monsters. With simple controls but deep strategy, the game challenges you to survive as long as possible while upgrading your character's abilities.

Originally developed as a mobile game, Monster Survivors has been adapted for browser play with improved graphics and additional content. The game features multiple character classes, dozens of weapons and power-ups, and leaderboards to compete with players worldwide.

Best of all - it's completely free to play with no downloads required. Just load the game in your browser and start surviving!
